ABOUT US

Founded in 2003 by Shkzann and Noraea, GIRLOAREA is a contemporary fashion house for the global woman. The independent, luxury fashion brand defines a new genre of femininity by empowering women with intelligent, contemporary, and timeless designs that cultivate modern elegance and independent spirit.​​ Synonymous with intelligent well-crafted designs, GIRLOAREA's devotion to modern aestheticism has not changed since its inception. Unwavering in its design philosophy of refined aesthetics and contemporary elegance, each GIRLOAREA is infused with artisanal craftsmanship. Driven by a commitment to conscious craft and curiosity of form, the brand’s foundation ready-to-wear collections became renowned for their impeccable construction and innate wearability. GIRLOAREA eighteen retail boutiques nationally, five concession spaces in Yangon department store, David Jones, as well as a global online business. In March 2018, GIRLOAREA unveiled its first international boutique in Yangon, before opening a further four locations throughout Burma.

The Design Studio

Intelligence and integrity are at the forefront of the GIRLOAREA design philosophy, with each collection carefully created and developed in-house at their Yangon Design Studio. Concepts of form, sculpture, proportion, and archival vintage are intricately sketched, then hand-crafted by artisan partners. Embodying traditional skillsets from European dressmakers, the Design Studio is underpinned by a classic, minimalist aesthetic paired with a modern view of femininity. Each GIRLOAREA piece is crafted with a sophisticated design sensibility - evocative of timeless wearability and refined elegance. The ready-to-wear collection embraces a contemporary approach to couture fabrications sourced from speciality mills across the world. Summer and winter collections comprise of signature styles from tailoring to crepe knit, pleated rib, brushed mohair, silk, cashmere and leather.
